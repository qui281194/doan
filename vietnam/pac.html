Pac Bo relic site in Cao Bang

On the occasion of the 76th anniversary of the August Revolution and the National Day of September 2, the Ethnic and Development Newspaper introduces readers to the Pac Bo historical relic - a place associated with Uncle Ho's activities during the first period of returning to his homeland. revolutionary religion (1941-1945). This relic complex is located in Truong Ha commune, Ha Quang district, Cao Bang province.


Pac Bo historical site is located in Truong Ha commune, Ha Quang district, 52 km north of Cao Bang city center.

Pac Bo Special National Monument (Truong Ha commune, Ha Quang district, Cao Bang) is the place where President Ho Chi Minh chose as the revolutionary leadership base (period 1941-1945) after more than 30 years of wandering. find a way to save water. After 77 years, this place has always kept intact traces of his life and revolutionary career.


Visiting and experiencing this place, visitors not only have the opportunity to learn about an important period in the active life of President Ho Chi Minh and the Vietnamese revolution, but also have the opportunity to admire the wonderful natural picture. pretty.


Lenin stream is called by local people as Giang stream or Trung stream

The stream of Lenin is as clear as a winding silk strip at the foot of the majestic Mack Mountain. The peaceful jade-green stream flows from the source of Pac Bo, known by the local people as Giang stream or Trung stream. During the days of living and working here, Uncle Ho's footprints were printed everywhere. Visitors are impressed with Coc Bo cave, where Uncle Ho's wooden board rests, and there is a fire to warm the cold cave or a stone table where Uncle sits to work, the rock he sits on fishing for...


In addition to outdoor monuments, visitors can offer incense at the temple of President Ho Chi Minh; visit the gallery to better understand the revolutionary movement of Cao Bang, as well as the revolutionary leadership of Uncle Ho after returning to the country through the narration of the guide at the relic site.


Temple of President Ho Chi Minh at Pac Bo relic site, Cao Bang province

Over the years, the infrastructure system for the development of tourism services here has been invested in a spacious and synchronous direction, creating more highlights for visitors to experience. Cao Bang province has invested, completed and put into use 6 projects at Pac Bo Special National Historic Site with a total investment of nearly 80 billion VND.


Specialty beef and vegetables in Cao Bang

Coming to Pac Bo, visitors can also shop for local products at the stalls of local people. Products imbued with the flavor of the mountainous region of Cao Bang such as: banh xeo, sli, shiitake mushrooms, vermicelli, dried bamboo shoots, wild vegetables... Local agricultural products not only serve the needs of customers. sightseeing, but also improve the material life of people in the region, while preserving and promoting tourism, and developing socio-economic for the locality.

In the tourism development strategy of Cao Bang province, Pac Bo tourist area is also oriented to become a key tourist destination. Over the past time, the Relic Management Board has also coordinated with local authorities to propagate and mobilize more than 100 households in the area to participate in protecting and promoting the value of the monument.
