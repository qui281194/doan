Admire the beauty of Machu Picchu

Peru - a country located on the South American continent, where there are many mysterious unsolved monuments and among them is Machu Picchu, which is called the "Lost City of the Incas". Machu Picchu is actually a well-preserved pre-Columbian Inca ruins at an altitude of 2,430 m on a spiky mountain. Machu Picchu is located in the Urubamba Valley in Peru, they seem to have been forgotten by the outside world for centuries, for quite a long time.


By 1911, archaeologists had discovered this place and this place became an interesting and mysterious destination, which is desired by experienced enthusiasts to be touched once. Let's find out about this place with the system of cheap air tickets.


The whole city of Machu Picchu has 140 different buildings, including temples, shrines, parks and houses built extremely solidly without any iron or mortar, only layers of hewn stone. Cutting together and closely to prevent earthquakes from destroying the city shows the transcendence of structure and aesthetics, this is also the question that has caused headaches for scientists during the past time. The buildings, giant walls were built on the terraced ground, the original purpose was for the nobles to rest, escape the busy city full of fatigue.


The ancient citadel of Machu Picchu is based on the mountain position and faces the river on three sides, as if there is no way up. The rooms are built in the middle of the mountain, in which there are hundreds of perfect natural stone columns, in the city there are many architectural styles such as: temple of the sun god, altar, king's palace, noble residence, residence of the people. common or slave, trade guilds, markets, and most especially perhaps fountains connected by channels and water pipes turbid in rock, etc. Those who have ever set foot here are amazed and not I can't believe there is such a masterpiece on such heights.


It is worth mentioning that the scenery around the holy place of Machu Picchu can not help but make people admire, the majestic mountains and forests, the higher they look down, the deeper valleys covered with young grass, each winding step. It is beautiful, a few herds of dwarf camels are wandering around grazing, on the citadel all year round, clouds are covered with ancient Inca architecture.


This place seems to be timeless with time, the ancient guard posts of the Inca soldiers, the arches lost in the wilderness... all the beautiful scenes just pass before your eyes, majestic, spectacular and ancient creating a scene. The image is extremely fanciful and mysterious as its origin was formed.


Standing on the ancient citadel of Machu Picchu, visitors will feel many emotions take turns rolling in their minds, which is surprise at first sight, followed by admiration for the talented hands of the ancient Incas, then That feeling of mysterious curiosity invaded every thought, and then suffocated with the majestic landscape of nature, interspersed with unique stone constructions. All huddled quietly under the sunshine over hundreds of years, allowing the green grass to nourish and protect from the erosion of time. Since 1983, this site has been selected as a UNESCO World Heritage site.


Seen from above, the Inca ruins become even more massive and extremely flashy, this is what remains of the most mysterious civilization of mankind. Coming to Machu Picchu, visitors can enjoy the majestic feeling of the vertical cliffs with an altitude of 600 m, breathe in the most primitive fresh air of nature, because there are very few people here, only birds. day and night hovering under the wonderful blue sky. Indeed, tourists who love novelty cannot ignore this place.
